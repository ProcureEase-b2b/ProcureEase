Frequently asked questions

Is the free plan really free?
Yes, completely free with no credit card required. The free plan includes up to 3 suppliers, 5 purchase orders, 10 inventory items, and 3 AI messages per day.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, with no questions asked.
How are payments processed?
All payments are handled by Paddle, our trusted payment partner. We accept all major credit and debit cards as well as many local payment methods. ProcureEase never stores your card details.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. Contact procureease@outlook.com within 7 days of any charge for a full refund, no questions asked.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell or share your data with third parties. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.
Does it work for businesses outside the US?
Absolutely. ProcureEase supports multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, BDT, INR, JPY and AED. It is used by businesses in over 40 countries.
